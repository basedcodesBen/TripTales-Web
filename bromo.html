Bromo Mountain

Bromo Mountain, located in East Java, Indonesia, is one of the most iconic and picturesque volcanoes in the world. Known for its stunning sunrise views, Bromo offers a unique and breathtaking landscape. The mountain is part of the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park, which also includes other mountains like Semeru and Batok. Visitors can hike to the summit of Bromo to witness the active crater and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding region.

How to Get There

To reach Bromo, you can take a flight to Surabaya or Malang and then continue by car or bus to Cemoro Lawang, the nearest village to the volcano. From Cemoro Lawang, you can arrange a jeep tour to the viewpoint to catch the sunrise and then explore the crater.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Bromo is during the dry season, from May to September, when the weather is clear and the sunrise views are spectacular. However, Bromo can be visited year-round, and each season offers a unique experience.

Things to Do

  • Witness the sunrise from the viewpoint at Penanjakan
  • Hike to the crater of Bromo
  • Explore the Sea of Sand (Pasir Berbisik)
  • Visit nearby attractions like Madakaripura Waterfall
